Title: Aircraft Maintenance Engineer

Responsibilities:
Responsible for regular inspection and maintenance of aircraft components such as engines and airframes to ensure optimal performance of the aircraft.

Skills Required:
- Familiarity with aircraft maintenance procedures
- Possession of a valid A&P (Airframe and Powerplant) license
- Background in mechanical engineering
- Knowledge of propulsion systems

Experience Required:
- Minimum of 10 years of experience in aircraft maintenance
- Experience with large commercial aircraft is preferred
